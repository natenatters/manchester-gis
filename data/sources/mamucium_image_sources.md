## LIDAR & Terrain

### Free LIDAR Data

1. **Environment Agency Open Data**
   - https://data.gov.uk
   - Search: "LIDAR Manchester"
   - 1m resolution DTM/DSM available for England

2. **LiDAR Finder** (Browser-based viewer)
   - https://www.lidarfinder.com/
   - Enter coordinates: 53.475624, -2.253643
   - Toggle between DTM (terrain only) and DSM (with buildings)

3. **CEDA Archive**
   - https://catalogue.ceda.ac.uk/uuid/acfa8955414aef710105ef640802b9aa/
   - 1m resolution for cities including Manchester

### Terrain for Cesium

- Environment Agency LIDAR can be converted to Cesium terrain tiles
- Historic England guide: https://historicengland.org.uk/content/docs/research/using-ea-lidar-data-pdf/

## Comparable Site Images (for Reference)

### Reconstructed Roman Forts

1. **Saalburg** (Germany) - Most complete reconstruction
   - Official site: https://www.saalburgmuseum.de/
   - Excellent gate and wall reference

2. **Arbeia** (South Shields) - Reconstructed gate & barracks
   - https://arbeiaromanfort.org.uk/
   - Similar auxiliary fort scale

3. **Vindolanda** - Timber phase reference
   - Well-preserved organic remains
   - Turf wall construction examples

4. **Lunt Roman Fort** (Coventry) - Reconstructed timber gateway
   - Good reference for wooden phase

### Academic Reconstruction Images

- "Plan of a Typical Roman Fort" - World History Encyclopedia
  - https://www.worldhistory.org/image/5989/plan-of-a--typical-roman-fort/
